Research

Discrete structures for reliable information storage.

My work studies mathematical structures behind reliable storage and communication. The main themes include error-correcting codes, extremal and probabilistic combinatorics, graph-theoretic methods, and information-theoretic models motivated by distributed storage and DNA storage systems.

Coding Theory

Codes for insertions, deletions, duplications, reconstructions, and related channels.

Extremal Combinatorics

Set systems, graph methods, finite-field problems, and Bollobás-type theorems.

DNA Storage

Combinatorial and coding-theoretic models for reliable molecular data storage.

Distributed Storage

Fractional repetition codes, access balancing, repair schemes, and storage reliability.
