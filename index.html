Research

Discrete structures for reliable information storage.

My research studies mathematical structures behind reliable storage and communication. I work on coding theory, extremal and probabilistic combinatorics, graph theory, and information-theoretic models motivated by distributed storage and DNA storage systems.

Coding Theory

Error-correcting codes, sequence reconstruction, insertion/deletion channels, duplication errors.

Extremal Combinatorics

Set systems, intersection theorems, Bollobás-type inequalities, finite-field extremal problems.

Storage Systems

Distributed storage, fractional repetition codes, access balancing, DNA storage models.

Information Theory

Capacity problems, combinatorial channel models, graph-theoretic information structures.
